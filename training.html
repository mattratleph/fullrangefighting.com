I got my start teaching the martial arts in the spring of 1992 and I have been sharing it, to the best of my ability, ever since.

The system that I teach is presented in phases or catagories of study. The following information is a partial overview of the training curriculum. This will also show the progression of arts and instruction. By no means does this encompass the entirety of the program.

  • The first category and phase of study covers the stand-up combat arts utilizing the existing tools of the body. This includes, but is not limited to, Kickboxing, Trapping, and Close Quarter Combat.
  • The second category and phase of study covers the close quarter weapons arts. This includes, but is not limited to, Stick fighting, Knife fighting, Short Sword, and other categories of weapons drawing from the Filipino Martial Arts. These arts are used to further develop empty hands skills as well as combative knowledge and awareness.
  • The third category and phase of study covers the area of Ground fighting which includes the style of Brazilian Jiujitsu.

All classes are taught from my fully equipped home gym. Multiple bags, mitts, gloves, weapons, etc. are available for training.

Note: I have an age criteria of 19 years and up, therefore, I do not teach young teens or children at this time.
