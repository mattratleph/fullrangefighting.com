I got my start teaching the martial arts in the spring of '92 and have been sharing it, to the best of my ability, ever since.

The system that I teach is presented in phases or catagories of study. Below I have listed a partial overview of my program agenda. This will also show the progression of arts and instruction.

I do have a 19 years and up age criteria, therefore, I do not teach young teens or children at this time.

  • The first catagory and phase of study: Includes, but is not limited to, Kickboxing, Trapping, and Close Quarter Combat.
  • The second catagory and phase of study: Includes, but is not limited to, Stick fighting, Knife fighting, and other categories of weapons drawing from the Filipino Martial Arts.
  • The third catagory and phase of study: Is the study of Ground fighting which does include Brazilian Jiujitsu.

All classes are taught from my home gym which is fully equipped for everything needed for training (multiple bags, mitts, gloves, weapons, etc.).
