I offer a variety of class options to give you the best possible training experience. Each option caters to your preference of working privately or with others.

  • Private: Classes are taught in a one on one setting. This allows for my full attention to a student's performance and growth without distraction.
  • Semi-Private: Classes are taught in a two on one setting. This allows for the development of training partners for study and practice outside of the class while still maintaining the high level of attention to each student's performance and growth.
  • Small Group: Classes are taught in a three (or more) on one setting. This allows for students who wish to study together with more partners. This can also translate into a Workshop setting.

Target workshops focused on specific studies and weekend training programs are also offered.
