Welcome to Full Range Fighting!

The official website of Rafael De La Fuente A.K.A Rif. I am a Certified Instructor and have been teaching Martial Arts for 20 years. My Combat background is based in Jeet Kune Do/Jun Fan Kickboxing, Filipino Martial Arts & Ground-fighting. The name Full-Range Fighting refers to the various ranges of combat in Empty hands and Weapons training. I am located in Romeoville, IL a S.W. suburb of Chicago.
